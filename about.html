More on Anthony Albano DPT

Find The Value in Movement

Dr. Anthony offers 1-on-1 physical therapy and prevention/recovery services to patients of all ages and capabilities. Unlike most physical therapy clinics that are crowded with patients, you have the opportunity to work with one health care professional providing full attention. This allows for a thorough evaluation process, monitoring of conditions and changes in conditions, and proper execution of exercises. This model allows for more attention to detail, reduced number of visits, and reduced overall cost of treatment.

Dr. Anthony is located inside WNY Strength and Conditioning which is a facility focused on weight training, athletic development, and healthy movement habits located in Depew. The facility is a fully equipped gym that allows Dr. Anthony access to equipment for physical therapy and prevention services. This arrangement allows him to expand his exercise selection to help you resolve your limitations and get back to the activities you want to do.

Dr. Anthony's approach to physical therapy is to look at the body as a whole system to establish a pattern of position and movement, address the normal asymmetries of the body, assess any spinal involvement of symptoms, then narrow down the specifics of the joints/soft tissues that may be affecting you. He uses a methodical treatment approach with exercise as the emphasis so you are empowered to treat yourself with less reliance on the healthcare professional. The goal is a strategy to help fit your needs so you are given a unique experience and individualized program. His primary strategies of treatment are through the Postural Restoration Institute and Mechanical Diagnosis and Treatment (McKenzie).
